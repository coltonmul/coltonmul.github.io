The system

Three levels. One direction: down.

Same numbering as always — 1 to 1000. The level tells you how deep the water is before you jump.

Level 1

Warm Up

Icebreakers with actual flavor. Zero risk, easy laughs, gets everyone talking in the first five minutes.

"What show could you start over and rewatch right now?"

Level 2

Go Deeper

Habits, history, opinions you actually hold. The middle depth where real conversation starts.

"What do you need less of in your life right now?"

Level 3

All In

The heavy stuff. Fear, regret, clarity, truth. For rooms with trust already in the bank.

"What truth about being human took you the longest to accept?"
Ways to run it

Four plays for your next meeting.

The same four formats you already use — now named, so you can call the play instead of explaining the rules.

01
Opener

Pick Your Number

Everyone calls a number from 1 to 1000, answers in one sentence or one minute. Fast, fair, zero prep.

02
Depth for time

Choose Your Level

Each member picks a level and gets that many minutes to answer whatever they're served. Depth costs time.

03
Pass it on

Hot Potato

Pull a question, hear it, then hand it to the member of your choice. Nobody hides in the back row.

04
One for all

One Question, Every Voice

One member picks the question. Every member gets one minute on it. Best for comparing where people really stand.
